Title: Pancake

Ingredients:

  • 15 dkg flour
  • 1 egg
  • 3 dl milk
  • 1 dl soda
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Oil for frying

1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, add the sugar, salt, and whisk with a whisk.

2. Add the flour and 2 dl of milk to the eggs, and whisk until smooth.

3. When it is almost lump-free, gradually dilute it with the remaining milk and soda.

4. Let the pancake batter rest for at least half an hour.

5. After resting, stir the batter, it should have the consistency of cream.

6. Heat a little oil in a pan, spread it over the surface of the pan with a spatula.

7. Pour a ladleful into the pan and swirl to cover.

8. Flip the pancake when edges lift.

9. Cook the other side. Stack on a plate.
